PHILADELPHIA COOLING MAP

SYX & SYT STUDIO™
This project takes Climate Change and Urban Heat Island as background. It seeks to show the hot spots in terms of surface temperature in Philadelphia, and directs residents to nearby cooling centers during extreme heat events. Start with press the "HEAT MAP" button or "COOLING CENTERS" button to see maps showing Philadelphia's surface temperature conditions, or cooling centers around you. For cooling centers, you can also filter and get routes.
